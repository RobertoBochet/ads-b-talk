DigitalDownConverter

Exploiting a digital mixer and a digital oscillator
the DDC shifts the sampled signal to lower frequencies...

DigitalDownConverter

...a digital low-pass filter removes
the useless frequencies
and a downsampler reduces
the amount of samples per second

The samples are sent in real time to a computer
through a hardware interface (e.g. USB)

Signal demodulation and decoding are demanded
to software running on the computer
